# How Our OpenClaw Agent 'Eddie' Helps Us Make $70k/mo with B2C Apps

> Ernesto Lopez's step-by-step case study: OpenClaw agent "Eddie" automates faceless content creation and influencer outreach for Prayer Lock app, converting agency costs into passive automation.

## Context

- Ernesto owns 11 apps generating $73k+/mo combined
- OpenClaw is used for **1 of the 11 apps**: Prayer Lock (Christian devotional app)
- Before Eddie: competitor Bible Mode spent $30k/mo on a content agency to manage 10+ slideshow accounts posting 3x/day
- After realizing OpenClaw could automate the same content pipeline: "That was the day Eddie was born"

## What Eddie Does

### 1. Faceless Content Factory

**The formula:**
> Same hook. Same framework. New words.

- Generates still image + text overlay content (slideshow format)
- Trained on top-performing hooks in the niche
- Training toward branded faceless accounts with recurring characters (converts "astronomically better")
- Connected to a posting tool that auto-posts across 4 accounts simultaneously
- Uses the **Larry skill** (by another creator) — 8M+ views generated via automated content

**Key insight:** A pink AI character alone was farming 50M+ views — all AI-generated, now automatable with OpenClaw.

**CTA tip:** Include app store screenshot in last slide, or text reference to app in one slide.

### 2. Influencer Outreach Automation

The full influencer process (which cost Ernesto $50k+ to crack):

1. Reaching out to influencers
2. Introduction + gathering information
3. Negotiating a CPM deal
4. Contract signing + onboarding
5. Content alignment training
6. Follow-up to ensure consistent posting

**Eddie has automated 60% of this.**

**How:**
- Given access to Prayer Lock's Instagram account
- Given parameters: Christian creators, 10k–50k followers, avg views 10k+
- Built a custom skill with step-by-step instructions
- Eddie doomscrolls Instagram, finds matching influencers, scrapes emails from bios
- Auto-sends 1,000 emails/day + 100 DMs/day to qualifying influencers
- Floods their inbox with pre-qualified leads

**Return:** 3x–4x consistently from influencer channel; one influencer alone generated significant revenue in 14 days.

## The Growth Strategy (All 11 Apps)

4 content formats used across the portfolio:
1. **Faceless slideshow content** — high volume, low cost, automatable
2. **UGC content** — authentic, converts well
3. **Influencer content** — highest ROI when system is cracked
4. **Spark ads** — amplify winning organic content

OpenClaw handles format #1 and #3 automation. Formats #2 and #4 are still manual.

## Key Lessons

> "Before you give OpenClaw a system to automate, you must first have the system built out for yourself."

This directly echoes 劳伦斯's point: OpenClaw amplifies working systems — it doesn't create them.

- First app launched: $20k in month 1 from just 2 influencer videos at 90% profit margin
- Content factory without Eddie: too hard to stay consistent
- Content factory with Eddie: consistent, scalable, passive

## Relevance for Workshop

Strong real-world case study showing:
1. **Specific, measurable ROI** — $30k/mo agency cost → $0 + automation
2. **Custom skill creation** — built a skill for influencer discovery with exact parameters
3. **Tool integration** — email access, Instagram access, posting tools
4. **The prerequisite principle** — "have the system first, then automate it"
