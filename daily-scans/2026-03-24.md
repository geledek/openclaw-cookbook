## What Changed vs. Recent Days

**Enterprise legitimacy is accelerating fast.** Three signals converging this week:
1. **Government-level governance** — China's security guidance is the first national framework. Expect other countries to follow.
2. **Big tech ecosystem play** — Nvidia (NemoClaw), Tencent (WeChat ClawBot), Baidu, and now Anthropic/Perplexity/Snowflake all positioning around OpenClaw. It's becoming a platform standard, not just a project.
3. **Breaking changes in core** — The 2026.3.22-beta.1 release is removing legacy paths (Chrome relay) and consolidating around ClawHub. The platform is maturing and shedding early-stage flexibility for stability.

**Repeated theme:** Security/governance is now the #1 conversation, overtaking "cool demos" from prior weeks. Workshop content should lean harder into enterprise security posture and deployment governance.
